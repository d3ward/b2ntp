How it works
Start searching by typing in the bar .
If what you are looking for is among your bookmakrs, it will be highlighted.
If you do not want to open the highlighted favorite press space twice and then enter, in this way you will search on the default search engine that you have set above in the setup.
Putting a first letter that corresponds to a shortcut and after a space writing what you want to search, you will start a search on the shortcut that you have chosen.
Once you get used to it, everything will be more productive !
